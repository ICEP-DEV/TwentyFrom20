Intervarsity Hackathon Series

Project Leader: Mr. Mash Phurutsi

Our collective goal is to foster digital solutions with a social impact,
particularly within educational spheres.
This three-day event will take place in 3 different provinces between September and October.
Approximately 500 students from various faculties, campuses,
and external universities will engage in this challenge.
This event not only introduces neighboring high schools to diverse opportunities in technology
but also underscores how entrepreneurship, the arts, and engineering contribute to innovative digital solutions.
It showcases the breadth of career paths within these fields and encourages students
to tackle real-life challenges posed by industry.
